Services We Offer

Iron Horse Asset Recovery delivers professional repossession services throughout Texas. Our operations are supported by modern technology, trained professionals, and strict compliance with industry regulations.

Our Core Services

  • Vehicle Repossession: Safe, legal, and professional recovery of vehicles of all types, including autos, trucks, and recreational units.
  • Skip Tracing & Asset Location: Nationwide location tools and databases to identify and recover hard-to-find assets.
  • Secure Storage: Recovered assets are protected in our fully secured and monitored storage facility.
  • Transport & Delivery: Efficient asset transport to designated locations for lenders, agents, or auctions.
  • Voluntary Surrenders: Coordination and handling of voluntary turn-ins to protect lender relationships.
